Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Crafted for Today

Founded in 2018, Terra Clay began as a small studio in the hills of Rajasthan, where ancient pottery traditions meet contemporary design sensibilities.

Each piece is handcrafted by our team of skilled artisans, using locally sourced clay and traditional techniques passed down through generations.
